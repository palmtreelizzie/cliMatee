Community

Protecting our home starts with you. Here are small things you can do in your community to help end the climate crisis:

  • Advocate! Spread the message and influence others to take action too.
  • Reuse, reduce, recycle!!
  • Shop and eat sustainably and wisely. (learn more about smart seafood choices here)
  • Conserve water and volunteer (click for volunteer opportunities near you here!)
  • Bike more. DRIVE LESS
  • Use less plastic and more reusable items! (plus hydroflasks and reusable bags look better than plastic bottles and bags :) )
  • These simple ideas can reduce your carbon footprint on the world.

Want to help? Find out how you can contribute. Ask and answer questions!
